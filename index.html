    how to use
    1. Scan — drop a receipt, click "Scan Receipt", or try an example below.
    2. Review — check the extracted fields and correct anything the scanner missed.
    3. Save — click "Save Receipt" to queue it. The 🧾 counter top-right tracks your total.
    4. Repeat — scan your next receipt; each save adds to the queue.
    5. Export — tap the 🧾 counter or the "Download CSV" button to export all saved receipts at once.
    tips for scanning
    • Use even lighting or flash — coloured or dim light causes OCR errors
    • Still struggling? Save the photo, boost contrast in your photo app, then scan the saved file
